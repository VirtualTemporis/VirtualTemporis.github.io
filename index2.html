General presentation

  • The game is an action game that can be played solo (1 player) or in duo, coop (2 players).
  • In this game, you will play as a scientist who has caused a temporal disruption, to solve it, she will have to navigate winding levels with a multitude of possible paths.
  • As she progresses in the game, she will receive different skills and weapons allowing her to better face the danger.
  • In addition, with each death, reality will become more and more unstable, the screen will blur until nothing can be seen, announcing your final death.
